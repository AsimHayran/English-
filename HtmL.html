Structure and templates for persuasive letters

Format Reminder:
  • Sender's address (skip in exam)
  • Recipient's name and title (e.g. Councillor Smith)
  • Date (optional in exam)
  • Greeting (e.g. Dear Councillor Smith,)
  • Intro paragraph
  • Main argument paragraphs (PEEL/PEAL structure)
  • Closing paragraph
  • Sign-off (Yours sincerely / Yours faithfully)
